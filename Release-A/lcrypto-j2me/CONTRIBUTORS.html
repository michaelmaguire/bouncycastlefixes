The following organisations and people have contributed to the Bouncy Castle Cryptography Package.

Thanks, may your castles never deflate!

Organisations

  • TU-Darmstadt, Computer Sience Department, RBG, for the initial lightweight TLS implementation, which is based on MicroTLS. MicroTLS was developed by Erik Tews at TU-Darmstadt, Computer Science Department, RBG under the supervision of Dipl.-Ing. Henning Baer and Prof. Max Muehlhaeuser. More Informations on MicroTLS can be found on: http://www.rbg.informatik.tu-darmstadt.de/MicroTLS/
